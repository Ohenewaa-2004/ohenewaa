MY JOURNEY WITH freeCode Camp

How it started

I started my freeCode Camp Journey on October 7,2024. It was enjoyable and fun in the beginning as I was learning something new. However, as time went the reality of the work hit me... It wasn't just something I was just to do for fun but I had to learn, retain and reproduce. I successfully completed the first module,"Responsive web" on November 15,2024.


What was required of me

I was required to host at least one of my projects on the freeCode camp as proof that I actually did what was required of me. I used a free hosting website called "tiiny" for that and my work was really really live. Boy!, you should see my excitement when that worked.
